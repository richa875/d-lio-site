Our Story

D’lio was born out of a desire to slow down. We believe your morning ritual should nourish you — without the caffeine crash. Made from roasted dandelion root, our blend offers a rich, earthy flavor that supports digestion, balance, and calm focus.

Why Dandelion?

Dandelion root has long been used as a herbal remedy to support liver health, digestion, and calm energy. It's a naturally caffeine-free alternative that mimics the roasted richness of coffee, without the crash.
